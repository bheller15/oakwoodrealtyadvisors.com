What We Do

Bridging Institutional Sponsors & Private Capital

Oakwood acts as General Partner, forming SPVs that allow our investor base to participate alongside institutions in high-quality real estate transactions. We source, structure, and manage each investment from inception through exit.

Deal Sourcing & Structuring

We partner directly with institutional-quality developers and operators who typically raise capital exclusively from large institutions — giving our investors access they could not otherwise obtain.

SPV Formation & GP Management

Oakwood forms and manages the special purpose vehicle, serving as General Partner. Investors participate as Limited Partners with full reporting through our institutional investor portal.

Investor Relations & Reporting

All capital calls, distributions, and portfolio reporting are delivered through Agora, an institutional-grade investor portal used by leading private equity real estate firms.

Aligned Incentive Structure

Our economics are performance-based. Investors receive an 8% preferred return before Oakwood participates in profits, ensuring our incentives are fully aligned with investor outcomes.

Our Approach

Disciplined. Selective. Aligned.

We focus on a narrow set of institutional-quality sponsors with proven track records across market cycles — enabling us to move quickly when opportunities arise and provide investors with conviction-driven exposure.

01
Sponsor Selectivity

We work exclusively with sponsors who have deep institutional track records, integrated platforms, and a history of delivering for their capital partners across market cycles.

02
Risk-Managed Structures

Each investment is structured to minimize execution risk — including Guaranteed Maximum Price contracts that transfer hard cost overrun exposure to the general contractor.

03
Investor-First Economics

Our LP-friendly waterfall features an 8% preferred return before any GP promote — ensuring investors are prioritized at every stage of the capital structure.
